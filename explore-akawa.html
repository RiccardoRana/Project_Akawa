Akawa is a project born in 2017 by the Dune group. It is an urbanized city in the desert that aims to host tourists for a shorter or longer time
and offer them the best attractions of the place as if they were inhabitants and not tourists. Akawa is one of the most sustainable urban communities
in the desert. It is made up of a rapidly growing clean-tech cluster, business free zone and residential neighbourhood with restaurants, shops and public
green spaces and famous “haima” when you can stay during your trip.
