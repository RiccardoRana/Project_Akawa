OUR SERVICES

Jeep or quad

We offer both quad and jeep rides. You can choose based on your preferences if you want to ride with an experienced driver or if you prefer to drive yourself. Remember that we use only environmentally-friendly vehicles which are always insured. We recommend to book your spot before your trip starts, so you will have a fully-organized schedule. Rent your quad here to ride the golden dunes independently and enjoy a fun-filled trip but don't forget the equipment! Our guardians will guide you in the desert and you will be amazed by the fauna and flora of the place! The alternative is a Jeep excursion guided by an oasis guardian who will let you discover all the secrets hidden in the Akawa dunes. Our jeeps are the best in the whole area, if you are a lover of engines and the outdoors, don't miss this opportunity!

After pickup from your hotel, begin your 25-kilometer quad bike ride in the desert. Venture along paths, back roads and admire the enchanting desert and the desert population. Then, climb the dunes, cross the desert, and admire sensational landscapes directly from your quad bike. Then continue to the various local villages and enjoy some free time to explore the area and recharge. After that, drive back to the starting point, take in impressive views of the protected areas and the diverse flora that you are used to.

After breakfast, we head further down, with jeep or quad, in southern Jordan to visit other significant sites including Wadi Rum. Explore Wadi Rum on a 1.30 hour jeep ride into the Jordanian desert with our quad! Wadi Rum was best described by T.E. Lawrence as Vast, echoing and god-like, and by the locals as Valley of the Moon. And then, overnight in Wadi Rum.

The Grand Tour is one of the most appreciated and amazing off-road adventures in the desert, and will allow you to see all the best places here. After a short briefing, during which the guide will explain how to drive a quad, we will start going towards the dunes. The tour go on along the dunes, in “our” desert, in the list of the most beautiful villages in abroad. From here, we will ascend from 450 meters a.s.l up to 2000 meters a.s.l. driving through the dunes and many different landscapes.

To do the desert safari Akawa, we were picked up from the hotel in the early afternoon, in a white SUV, a Toyota Land Cruiser. Once in the car, reaching the desert is not the blink of an eye. It is separated by just over an hour from Akawa, but it is not until forty-five or fifty minutes when you start to see how the landscape becomes arider and arider until you leave the highway to enter a sandy road that ends in a camp from which the first dunes can be seen.
